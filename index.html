Key Learnings

In this assignment, I explored the efficacy of ResNet-18 and ResNet-50 architectures on the MNIST and Fashion-MNIST datasets. The experiments revealed that while both models achieved exceptional accuracy (>99%) on MNIST, Fashion-MNIST posed a greater challenge (~92%), with the Adam optimizer providing superior convergence and stability over SGD.

Interestingly, the lighter ResNet-18 model often performed comparably to ResNet-50 within limited epochs, demonstrating that increased model depth does not always guarantee significantly better performance for simpler datasets.
